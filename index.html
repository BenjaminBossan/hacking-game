How to Play

  • The Hacking Game is a puzzle game inspired by the Cyberpunk 2077 hacking minigame. You are a hacker trying to breach a secure system.
  • The board is a grid of tiles (7Ã—7 by default), with each tile showing a number between 1 and 9.
  • Begin by selecting a tile in the top row.
  • Your second move can be in the same row or column as your first; then moves alternate between horizontal and vertical.
  • You may only select a tile whose number is equal to or greater than your last selected tile.
  • You cannot select the same tile twice
  • The round ends when you select a tile in the bottom row.
  • Your score equals the sum of your selected tile numbers multiplied by the length of your path.
  • Each round has a required score that increases after every win.
  • If no legal move remains or time (45 seconds) expires, you lose the round and must restart.
  • If you lose, you can view the winning path using the "Show Winning Path" button.
  • Source code is available on GitHub.
