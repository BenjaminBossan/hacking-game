How to Play

Game Rules:

  • The board is a grid. Each tile shows a number (1–9).
  • Start by choosing a tile in the top row.
  • For your second move, choose a tile either in the same row or the same column as your first choice. Then moves alternate: if the previous move was horizontal, the next must be vertical, and vice versa.
  • You can only select a tile whose number is equal to or higher than the last selected tile.
  • The round ends when you select a tile in the bottom row.
  • Your score is computed as (sum of selected tile numbers) × (length of your path). You need to meet the required score to win the round.
  • If no legal move remains or time (60 seconds) runs out, you lose the round and must restart the game.
