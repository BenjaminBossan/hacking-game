How to Play

  • The Matrix Breach is a puzzle game inspired by the Cyberpunk 2077 hacking minigame. You are a hacker trying to breach a secure system.
  • The board is a 7Ã—7 matrix with each tile showing a number between 1 and 9.
  • Begin by selecting a tile in the top row.
  • Your second move can be in the same row or column as your first; subsequent moves alternate between horizontal and vertical.
  • You may only select a tile with a number equal to or greater than your last selected tile.
  • You cannot select the same tile twice.
  • You cannot end the level before reaching the minimum score.
  • Your score equals the sum of your selected tile numbers multiplied by the length of your path.
  • Once you reach the minimum score, you breach the matrix and may escape by selecting a tile in the bottom row.
  • Each round's required score increases after every win.
  • If no legal move remains or time expires, you lose the round and must restart.
  • If you lose, you can view the winning path using the "Show Solution" button.
  • Chase new high scores by winning consecutive rounds. The high score is the sum of each round's score.
  • Source code is available on GitHub.
