How to Play

Game Rules:

  • The board is a grid of tiles (7×7 by default), and each tile shows a number between 1 and 9.
  • Start by choosing a tile in the top row.
  • Your second move may be in the same row or same column as your first move. Then, moves must alternate between horizontal and vertical.
  • You can only select a tile whose number is equal to or greater than the last tile you selected.
  • Vertical moves can go upward or downward (provided the destination tile is unoccupied), but the sequence must eventually reach the bottom row.
  • The round ends when you select a tile in the bottom row.
  • Your score is calculated as the sum of selected tile numbers × by the length of your path.
  • Each round has a required score that you must meet or exceed, which increases after every successful round. It is shown at the bottom of the screen.
  • If no legal move remains or time (45 seconds) expires, you lose the round and must restart the game.
  • If you lose, you can view the winning path using the "Show Winning Path" button.
